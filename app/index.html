Welcome to Seabreeze Vacation Villas

Nestled in the heart of Bahama Reef Country Club, overlooking the turquoise Bahamian waters is Sea Breeze Vacation Villas. This exclusive boutique resort boasts fully furnished and tastefully appointed two bedroom villas within a secure gated community that offers a private and peaceful setting with only a 3-minute walk to the secluded beach. The condos are comfortable, spacious, and come with all of the modern conveniences of home, making it a great vacation home for couples or the entire family!

Marina

Cruise in from afar with your yacht, or sailboat. The property has 200’ of deep water canal, which can accommodate boats up to 60 ft, 6 ft draft, some floating docks available. Available services include fresh water hookup, 15/30/50 amp at 120 or 220 volts, 60hz electrical connection, wireless high speed internet, outside shower, full free laundry facilities, fresh water heated pool, large hot tub and barbeque for your enjoyment. For those living on board, the laundry, pool, BBQ and wifi can be accessed for a fee of $5 per day. The owner is a rescue EMT crew member of Bahamas Air Sea Rescue Assoc. Full boat and motor repair are available nearby.

Sea Breezes Marina is located on the south shore of Grand Bahama Island in Freeport between the Xanadu and Our Lucaya hotels. It is situated only 80 miles off the coast of Florida. Sea Breezes Marina is at the end of a low usage canal, offering a quiet nights sleep, with minimum waves; an ideal, low stress, relaxing spot for a long weekend getaway or for permanent dockage of your vessel. The marina is located just 1000 ft off the south shore and is part of the gated, quiet luxury resort. Jetty entrance to the marina is 6 ft. at MLT and 9 ft. at HT with no overhead obstructions. The marina is located at a canal junction, providing a large turn-around area. The entrance GPS co-ordinates to the marina are 26 29 22 N and 78 30 50 W. If you are interested in short term or long term dockage at Sea Breezes Marina, please email info@seabreezevacationvillas.com or call us at 242-351-1186/87/88 for additional Information.

Off-Site activities abound for those seeking an active vacation

Its a short, three minute walk to a large, flat, private, soft, white sand beach; ideal for swimming, sunning, snorkeling, or fishing, for bonefish on the beach or grouper and snapper off the rock jetty. For more adventure, try diving for lobster right off the very quiet, secluded beach. Rental fishing boats, with guide, fishing rods and bait, are available. Snorkel equipment and ocean kayaks are also available for rent. Scuba diving and snorkeling are some of the most popular activities, and the Bahamas boasts some of the best dive sites around. At the UNEXSO Dolphin Encounter, a swim with the friendly dolphins is a breathtaking experience to cherish! If you enjoy deep-water fishing, there are many rental boats, to go catch that elusive Grouper, Wahoo, Barracuda or other reef fish. Horseback riding on one of Grand Bahama Island's great beaches is a very special equestrian experience. Don't miss the 40-acre Lucayan National Park, famous for its limestone caves that you can easily explore on foot, and even dive when arranged through UNEXSO. Numerous championship golf courses challenge your golfing skills. Kayaking is easy and fun, and Grand Bahama Island's beautiful mangrove waterways and saltwater flats are a fantastic a place to learn. Guided tours offer numerous birding, fishing and botanical opportunities. Water Sports offer great cruises aboard various boats for beach parties, dinner cruises, sunset tours, and booze cruises. Water sport rentals also include sea-doos, banana boat rides and parasailing. And, if you have had too much sun, there’s the Port Lucaya Marketplace for great food and shopping, or the Treasure Bay Casino for indoor activity.
