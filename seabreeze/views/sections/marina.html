Sea Breezes Marina is located on the south shore of Grand Bahama Island in Freeport between the Xanadu and Our Lucaya hotels. It is situated only 80 miles off the coast of Florida. Sea Breezes Marina is at the end of a low usage canal, offering a quiet nights sleep, with minimum waves; an ideal, low stress, relaxing spot for a long weekend getaway or for permanent dockage of your vessel. The marina is located just 1000 ft off the south shore and is part of the gated, quiet luxury resort. Jetty entrance to the marina is 6 ft. at MLT and 9 ft. at HT with no overhead obstructions. The marina is located at a canal junction, providing a large turn-around area.
The entrance GPS co-ordinates to the marina are 26 29 22 N and 78 30 50 W.
